Parts @ OKC -- **Coordinating Shipment to TJX**
TJX Removal

New equipment must be installed at TJX to facilitate end-to-end connection to ZLA Comm Node 2. This equipment installation is delayed until the agreement between SENEAM and FAA can be updated. There is currently no timeline, but work is ongoing.

We will be coordinating installation of the equipment at TJX with local technician support from SENEAM and FTI. We will coordinate with the Operators to monitor the connectivity of the new equipment via the O&M Topology display. The cutover will progress as follows:

  1. (When the equipment arrives, prior to install) Site technicians at at least one of the MX sites manually reconfigure ACS-B routers using W7.297L
  2. Operators upgrade ZLA Core Router B
  3. Site Technician powers off TJX DST-B
  4. Site removes distribution node equipment and installs longport devices:
    • Connect cables
    • Apply power
    • Verify configuration
  5. Operators verify comm from upgraded MX sites
  6. Operators coordinate with remaining MX sites to manually upgrade ACS-B routers and verify comms

After the O&M upgrade to W7.296L, the TJX Distribution Node is no longer present on the O&M Topology. The O&M Topology now displays red comm lines between ZLA Core B and all MX sites, but the MX sites continue to use the legacy comms through TJX to accurately report their modes and status.
