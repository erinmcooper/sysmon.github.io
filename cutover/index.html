Parts being shipped to OKC
TJX Removal

New equipment must be installed at TJX to facilitate end-to-end connection to ZLA Comm Node 2. This equipment is projected to be installed by the end of June 2020; however, coronavirus may impact the ability to travel to Mexico to perform the work.

If the equipment cannot be installed by August 12, the ZLA Core Router B and O&Ms will be upgraded. At this point, all Mexico sites will lose comm on Ring 2 until the equipment can be installed at TJX and the Mexico WRS Site comms validated end-to-end.
