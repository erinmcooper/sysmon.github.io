Parts @ OKC -- **Coordinating Shipment to TJX**
TJX Removal

New equipment must be installed at TJX to facilitate end-to-end connection to ZLA Comm Node 2. This equipment is projected to be installed by the end of August 2020; however, coronavirus may impact the ability to travel to Mexico to perform the work.

We will be coordinating installation of the equipment at TJX with local technician support from SENEAM and FTI. We will coordinate with the Operators to monitor the connectivity of the new equipment via the O&M Topology display. The cutover will progress as follows:

  1. (While equipment is in transit to TJX) Site technicians at all MX sites manually reconfigure ACS-B routers using W7.297L
  2. (When the equipment arrives, prior to install) Operators upgrade ZLA Core Router B and POCC O&M to W7.296L
  3. NOCC powers off TJX DST-B

  4. Site removes distribution node equipment and installs longport devices:

    • Connect cables
    • Apply power
    • Verify configuration
  5. POCC O&M verifies comm from MX sites
  6. Operators upgrade NOCC O&M

If the equipment cannot be installed by September 12, the ZLA Core Router B and O&Ms will be upgraded. At this point, all Mexico sites will lose comm on Ring 2 until the equipment can be installed at TJX.
